Welcome! I am a second-year Finance PhD student at the University of Melbourne. I earned my bachelor's degree from the University of Melbourne and am now continuing my research here as a PhD student. My primary interests lie at the intersection of machine learning and finance, with a particular focus on applying natural language processing (NLP) techniques to financial applications. I explore how large language models (LLMs) can be leveraged for stock price prediction and earnings forecasting. Additionally, I investigate the limitations and biases of LLMs in finance and work on strategies to enhance their efficiency and reliability in practical use. I am also highly interested in unconventional data sources for economic and financial research, such as video analysis.
